[ STAND UP AND FIGHT GENDER EQUALITY TOGETHER ]

#CLOSETHEGAP

#CloseTheGap is a global movement launched by She Radio 99.6fm, Indonesia. We are aware that there remains a disparity in pay between men and women in many countries around the world. Women are paid less than men due to no attributable reason other than gender. This is gender discrimination and we must put a stop to it. Let’s stand together and make women’s voices be heard. Read on and see what you can do to be part of this movement.
